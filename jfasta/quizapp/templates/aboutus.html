About Jipime Fasta Team

Jipime Fasta is a free online solution for students who have to remember large amounts of information and assess their knowledge in multiple fields. You can easily finish your work by answering interactive questions about different topics or studies in your course. There is also an opportunity to compare your performance with students from across the country.

///////////////////

Jipime Fasta is here to help you succeed in your final exams and in your studies at school. We use interactive tests to prepare students for final exams and to help students study for exams and know what is expected of them. We also give you this information for your professors, so you know what your grade is expected to be and when you'll get your grade back.

////////////////////////////

Suppose you want to improve the grades of your students. If you believe you can give them a leg up with a well-fitting shoe, Jipime Fasta will be a powerful tool to help them. Jipime Fasta is an interactive tool that helps students prepare for multiple types of final exams like Physics, Chemistry and Biology study. Each test features various modes and questions that help students understand the content.
